About

ARMSFLOW is a data visualization which displays arms transactions globally between 1950 and 2006. It was created by Jeffrey Warren of Vestal Design with data from the Stockholm International Peace Research Institute.

If you have questions, suggestions, bugs to report, or would like to discuss other large data sets to visualize, please send an email.

ARMSFLOW includes 14,619 arms transactions (each is a sum of 1 year's exports) and 228 government entities.
